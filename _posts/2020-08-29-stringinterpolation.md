## Make strings easier to read going through code.

Simply by placing a dollar sign in front of the variabes declared below, Matt and Manchester, when
they are output in the console screen you can confirm what is being loaded in and output.


'''javascript
let myName='Matt';  
let myCity='Manchester';  
console.log(`My name is ${myName}. My favorite city is ${myCity}.`);
'''
